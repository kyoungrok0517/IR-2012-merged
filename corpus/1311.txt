We now rely on statistically proven medical practice, or what we usually call evidence-based medicine. You can log in acc.org o look at their guidelines. 

There are three accepted ways of treating coronary artery heart diseases: medically, angioplsty(stenting) and bypass surgery. 

If your disease is mild, medication is the first option. if it gets worse, angioplasty should be considered. Surgery is recommended only for those who arent amenable for medical or angioplasty treatment.
