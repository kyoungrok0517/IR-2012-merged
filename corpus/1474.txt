It's based on the 2006 movie Babel by Alejandro González Iñárritu.
In it several story lines are developed, one plays in Japan.
It tells the story of Chieko Wataya (Rinko Kikuchi), a rebellious, deaf-mute Japanese teenage girl, traumatized by the recent suicide of her mother.
In one of her self-destructive scenes she goes out 'commando', showing a group of boys the 'black hairy monster'.
