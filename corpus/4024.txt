 Cheese ham rolls

ingredients
100 gm hung curd 
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp cream 
3 tbsp ground pimento 
1 tbsp lemon juice 
1 tbsp vodka 
salt and pepper 
6 cooked asparagus spears 
6 thin slices ham 
2 pineapple slices 


method

Prepare filling by combining hung curd, butter, cream, pimento, lemon juice, vodka and seasoning. 

Spread over ham slices and place an asparagus spear over each slice. 

Roll up into logs, cover and chill. 

To serve, slice into Â½ " pieces. 

Secure with cocktail sticks and chunks of pineapple
