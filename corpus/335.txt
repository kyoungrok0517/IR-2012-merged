Most fad /quick results diets work in the short term because thay are basically dehydrating you, so yes a water fast is an effective weight-loss mechanism.  However, when you go back to normal food and water consumption, your body is rebuilding the supply of water that it lost during the diet and in some cases, more weight is put on than was lost in the first place.

Also, it is not good for gastro-intestinal health if you refrain from drinking water.  Nor is it good in other areas.  Lose too much water and you will land yourself in the hospital.  Please read the Wikipedia article linked below.
