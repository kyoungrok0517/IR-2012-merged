Babies shouldn't be given honey until over one year of age (botulism). They also shouldn't be given milk until one year of age because it can damage the lining of their intestines. Peanuts/peanut butter/anything with peanuts in it until they turn 3 years of age. Not only is it a choking hazard, but the chance of having an allergy to peanuts is greater under the age of 3.
