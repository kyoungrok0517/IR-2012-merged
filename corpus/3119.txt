BMI stands for Body Mass Index.  It gives you a number that tells you if you are at a healthy weight or not.  Use a search engine and type "BMI calculator" and it will help you get your number.  These numbers are used to classify people in to under weight,  normal weight, obese, morbidly obese.  These numbers can be very important for someone who wants to have gastric bypass.  They can also be important in helping people with eating disorders to stay within a healthy weight.
