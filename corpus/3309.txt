Uniformity in the learning environment takes away certain external social pressures which result from materialism.

It also is a matter of safety and promoting and environment soley geared towards learning. By introducing uniforms you are reducing the likelihood of a child carrying a concealed weapon to school, likewise you are reducing the likelihood that a child will be beat up, berated or otherwise tormented for not wearing the Name brand shirt.

First amendment rights to not pertain to dress, nor do they extend into a private forum like a school.
