1. The first advanced civilizations in Cambodia appeared in around 1,000 B.C. (that's definitely before 1953!)

2. The U.S. carried out bombing raids on Cambodia during 1973.

3. During the bombings, many civilians sought refuge in the capital, Phnom Penh.

4. Diabolical leader Pol Pot changed the name to Democratic Kampucha in 1975.

5. Pol Pot marched civilians out into rural areas and foced them to work in agriculture, with 11th century tools. They got rid of anything new or western, discarded medicine, and destroyed libraries and temples.

6. Almost 3 million civilians, mostly doctors, lawyers, teachers, and anyone with training were killed.
